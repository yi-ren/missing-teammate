Welcome to Missing Teammate!

Ever had a group project where one of your members went missing? Ordinarily, that member would have overslept after a night of playing Beer Pong with a group of his guy friends, and shown up a bit late to your meeting. Here, that is not the case. Join the remaining members of the group and help us find this guy. Our project depends on it!
